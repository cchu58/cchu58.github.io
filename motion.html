Adobe Animate

Here I'm showcasing my pixel final animated. I wanted to animate it with the feeling of a storybook. The background fades into view as the stitching border drops slowly into view. I timed it so that the type would transform as the border dropped into place. As the presser feet finish falling into place a fashion show with the models entering from the left and floating one element after another begin. I had the sound start to fade out in the last thirty seconds.

I'm pretty proud of this animation since I had no previous experience in animation. I used motion tweens for all the visual elements and shape tween for the type.
